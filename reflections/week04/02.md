## Questions

1. What are the three states of a `Promise`?

`A: Pending, resolved, and rejected.`

2. How do `promise`s seek to resolve the issues of "callback hell"?

`A: If we use promises, we can attach Callbacks rather than passing them, which solves this problem.`

3. What is the difference between `.then()` and `.catch()`?

`A: Then is used for resolved promises, catch is for if it gets rejected.`
